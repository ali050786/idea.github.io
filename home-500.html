OUR STORY
Idealab formed in 2019 with the vision of supplying modern design-led high-quality office furniture products within India. After months of market research in the furniture industry, It came to our attention that newer concepts were absent on the landscape of the interior designed arena. We also realised the absence of interesting concepts which can transform the space we live and work in. Wewanted to build a platform that would bring newer ideas of concepts in form of products and design experience to the end clients.what we do best; supplying high-quality modern office furniture to like minded creative businesses throughout the country.Idealab is a private enterprise run by passionate people who believewe can contribute positively to improving the working lives of peopleat the office
