Founded in 1964, ARTIS, Arquitectura Interior is a company specialised in design and installation of partititions and furniture for office space, corporate headquarters, business and contract centres.
FLATbyArtis is the modular partition system that allows customised distributions according to the needs of each project. The FLAT system includes partitions that are single glazed, double glazed, double blind panel, cabinet partition and with hinged and sliding doors; allowing spaces to be formed that express the same visual and technical language; harmony and modern office aesthetic and functional comfort.
